A small piece of heaven

Bite into satisfactions with our special puto, with its small rounded structure, and smooth texture, you could somehow feel a grasp of heaven in your mouth. Steamed patiently until tenderly soft, our product is guaranteed to bring a smile to your lips.

Made to order

To ensure that the steamed cakes that we will give you are 100% fresh, we make them upon the orders made. We avoid over stocking which may affect the product negatively. Please make your order in advance to give us enough time to properly prepare your delicacies. And rest assured that they will be delivered to you with the highest quality.

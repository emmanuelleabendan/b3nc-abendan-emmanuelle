Treat yourself with our cassava cake

Classic Filipino delicacy

A chewy and delicious delicacy that can satisfy anyone and everyone. The taste of coconut sport (macapuno), creamy toppings, and of course the cassava itself. A simple and humble dessert but is so good that it could easily compete with other expensive delicacies. If you haven’t tried a cassava cake before, we’re here to help you change that.

Made to order cassava cakes

To ensure that the cassava cakes that we will serve you are 100% fresh, we bake them upon the orders made. We avoid over stocking which may affect the product negatively. Please make your order in advance to give us enough time to properly prepare your cakes. And rest assured that they will be delivered to you with the highest quality.
